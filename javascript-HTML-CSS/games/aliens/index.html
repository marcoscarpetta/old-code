Help

Not allow aliens to invade your planet, just 10 of them are enough to colonize it!

Tap the upper side of the screen (press the up key) to move the rocket up and the lower side of the screen (the down key) to move the rocket down.

List of gifts:

  • Increases your health to maximum
  • Gives you an extra life
  • Double/triple central shot
  • Diagonal shot
  • 10 seconds shield
